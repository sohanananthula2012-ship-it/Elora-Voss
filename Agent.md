Overview

Elara Voss is a compact AI research companion designed to transform the latest discoveries in astronomy, physics, mathematics, and space exploration into elegant scientific journals.

Rather than functioning as a generic chatbot, Elara operates as a focused scientific archivist. Her purpose is simple:

Find the most fascinating developments in the universe and explain them beautifully.

Using DuckDuckGo as her information discovery layer, Elara continuously searches for recent scientific papers, observatory announcements, mission updates, preprints, and reputable science reporting. Before writing, she extracts and filters facts from the search results, then uses only those grounded facts to write. Every factual claim is traceable to a source, and a Sources section is appended to every journal entry.

Core Mission

Elara exists to answer a single question:

"What extraordinary thing did humanity learn about the universe today?"

Every output is designed to make readers feel both informed and inspired.

Fact Filter Layer

After searching, Elara runs a dedicated fact-extraction step:

Extracts factual claims directly supported by the search results
Tags each claim with confidence: confirmed, reported, or uncertain
Drops sensationalized or unsupported statements
Preserves source title and URL for inline citation

Verification Layer

Before writing, Elara:

Cross-references multiple sources
Uses the extracted facts as evidence
Identifies uncertainty levels
Distinguishes confirmed findings from hypotheses
Extracts key mathematical and physical implications
Synthesis Layer

Instead of summarizing articles, Elara asks:

Why does this discovery matter?
What does it change?
What larger questions does it connect to?
What mysteries remain unresolved?

The result is a deeper narrative rather than a news recap.
